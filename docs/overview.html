🚀 ArrivalX — Settlement Layer for the Agent Economy

x402-compatible · Multi-Jurisdiction Licensed · 5-line SDK

What is ArrivalX?

AI agents are effectively unbanked. They cannot open bank accounts, pass KYC, or authorize payments — yet they need to pay for API access, compute, data, and other agents.

ArrivalX is the licensed settlement infrastructure that solves this. Four live money transmission licenses (AU DCE, US MSB, HK MSO, CA MSB). Protocol-agnostic (x402, MPP, AP2). Compliance-first. 5 lines to integrate.
