What is ArrivalX?

AI agents are effectively unbanked. They cannot open bank accounts, pass KYC, or authorize payments — yet they need to pay for API access, compute, data, and other agents.

ArrivalX is the licensed settlement infrastructure that solves this. Four live money transmission licenses. Protocol-agnostic. Compliance-first. 5 lines to integrate.
